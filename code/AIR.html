Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NexusAir's baggage policy?

Economy Plus allows 23kg, Business Elite allows 40kg, and First Class offers unlimited baggage. Carry-on is always 7kg regardless of class. Special items can be pre-booked at no extra charge for First Class passengers.

Can I change or cancel my flight?

Yes! All NexusAir tickets can be modified up to 24 hours before departure. First Class tickets are fully refundable anytime. Business and Economy Plus have flexible modification fees. Use the NexusAir app or call our 24/7 concierge.

How does the AI Travel Advisor work?

Our Neural Travel AI analyzes your travel history, preferences, and seasonal patterns to recommend optimal destinations and booking windows. It monitors price fluctuations in real-time to alert you when fares drop below your target price.

How early should I arrive at the airport?

We recommend 2 hours for domestic and 3 hours for international flights. First Class passengers enjoy dedicated fast-track lanes and private check-in counters â€” you can typically complete the process in under 15 minutes.

Do you offer group or corporate bookings?

Absolutely. NexusAir offers dedicated group booking services for 10+ passengers with negotiated rates. Our corporate travel program provides assigned account managers, flexible invoicing, and priority support for business clients.
